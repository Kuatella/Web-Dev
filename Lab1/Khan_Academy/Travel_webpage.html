Almaty, the largest city in Kazakhstan, is a vibrant cultural hub nestled at the foothills of the Trans-Ili Alatau mountains. Boasting a rich history, stunning natural beauty, and a thriving arts scene, Almaty offers visitors a unique blend of urban sophistication and picturesque landscapes. Explore its diverse cuisine, bustling markets, and outdoor adventures to experience the essence of this captivating city.

Almaty Metro

Visitors can admire the unique architecture and artwork of each metro station while traveling between the city's attractions. The metro is clean, safe, and affordable, making it an ideal transportation option for visitors.
